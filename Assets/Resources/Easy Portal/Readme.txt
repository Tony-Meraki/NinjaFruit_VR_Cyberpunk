-------------
Easy Portal
-------------

A very easy to use portal. 

Contains:
- 1 portal prefab.
- 2 demo scenes.
- 3 textures & 3 materials.
- 1 script for demo scene 1 & 2.
- 1 night skybox for demo scene 1.
- Some additional particles for demo scene 1 & 2.

Please watch the video introduction (https://youtu.be/Lq4pn-kGp2o) to learn more about it.
